## Positioning

> **The fan-retention engine that turns every verified match update into the next meaningful action.**

The product loop is **Watch → Call → Earn → Open → Craft → Duel → Return**.
Do not introduce Final Whistle as a scores app, prediction game, NFT collection,
or card battler. The proof is one uninterrupted causal journey.

- The Question Engine is deterministic; production recap AI is disabled and is not a headline claim.
- Collectibles have verifiable lineage; they are not described as NFTs.
- Solana provides the embedded identity and optional proof anchoring.
- Duel axes are game attributes, not official performance ratings.
