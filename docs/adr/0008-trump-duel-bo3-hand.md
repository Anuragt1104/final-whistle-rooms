# Trump Duel is best-of-three with a three-card Hand

Trump Duel (default 1v1) is classic axis-comparison football cards, not simultaneous Snap-style play: each Fan selects a Hand of three Player Cards; duels are best of three Rounds; the Attacker picks the Axis; higher value wins; at most one Skill Card may be played per Round; Round winner becomes the next Attacker. Chosen over single-card showdowns (too thin) and full simultaneous bluff modes (more UI/rules cost) so the MVP stays demoable in under two minutes while matching Panini/Adrenalyn vernacular.
