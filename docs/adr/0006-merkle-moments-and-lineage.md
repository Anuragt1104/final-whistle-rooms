# Merkle-back Moments and playable Lineage only

Every minted Moment is a Merkle Leaf (event identity + Odds Sandwich summary + fixture scope). When a Pack or Craft creates a Player Card or Skill Card from Moment context, a Lineage leaf links that card to its parent Moment. Duel round-by-round clicks are not proven. Moment Arena proves the seed Moment, not every action. Optional Solana memo anchor remains a room/fixture-level root timestamp, not per-card mint spam.
