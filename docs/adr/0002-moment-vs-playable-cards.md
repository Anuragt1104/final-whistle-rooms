# Moments are memories; Player and Skill cards are playable

Significant Match Events mint Moments (collectible, shareable, Merkle-backed). Player Cards and Skill Cards are a separate playable layer used in Duels. Moments are not combat units. Live drops may grant playable cards during the same event stream, and Moments remain craft fuel — but the types stay distinct so collectible trust and game balance do not collapse into one object.
